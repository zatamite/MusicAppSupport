ViolinPro Support

AI Posture Coaching

ViolinPro uses advanced computer vision to analyze your playing posture. Ensure you are in a well-lit environment and the camera can see your full upper body. The analyzer checks for:

  • Shoulder and wrist alignment
  • Chinrest seating and head tilt
  • Bow arm mechanics and elbow height

Precision Tuner

The high-fidelity pitch engine provides real-time feedback down to the cent. Supports standard G-D-A-E tuning and historical baroque temperaments.
